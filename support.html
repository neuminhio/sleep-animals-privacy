üìä How is my sleep animal calculated?
Sleep Animals uses two key metrics:
  • Sleep Duration: Total hours of actual sleep (not just time in bed)
  • Sleep Efficiency: Percentage of time actually asleep vs. time in bed
Your daily animal is based on last night's sleep, while your weekly animal considers your 7-day average and consistency.
